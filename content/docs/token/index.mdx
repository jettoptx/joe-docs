**$JTX** is the JettChat access and governance token. It lives on **Solana mainnet** at:

```
9XpJiKEYzq5yDo5pJzRfjSRMPL2yPfDQXgiN7uYtBhUj
```

Standard SPL token, 6 decimals.

## Why a token gate

JettChat is gated to keep the network costly to abuse and aligned with users who want it to exist. Holding **1 JTX** is the lowest bar to entry, and that's by design — the gate is meant to filter automated abuse, not paywall users. Stripe and [Tempo CLI](https://github.com/jettoptx/tempo) fallbacks at $8.88 give wallet-less paths in.

## Four tiers, three of them stakeable

JettChat enforces four code-level tiers at the middleware. The top three map directly to the [staking tiers](/docs/token/tiers) on [astroknots.space/stake](https://www.astroknots.space/stake):

| Code tier | JTX held | Maps to |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| `basic` | ≥ 1 | Base entry — JettChat access (both modes) |
| `mojo` | ≥ 12 | [MOJO](/docs/token/tiers) subscription tier |
| `dojo` | ≥ 444 | [DOJO](/docs/token/tiers) subscription tier |
| `spaceCowboy` | ≥ 1,111 | [SPACE COWBOY](/docs/token/tiers) subscription tier |

The staking page is the **source of truth** for prices, durations, and unlocks. The code values mirror the staking model, not the other way around.

## Founder bypass

The protocol-treasury wallet `FEUwuvXbbSYTCEhhqgAt2viTsEnromNNDsapoFvyfy3H` is auto-tiered to `spaceCowboy` regardless of balance. This is documented in [On-Chain Addresses](/docs/getting-started/on-chain-addresses).

## Where $JTX is used

- **JettChat gating** — [JETT Auth](/docs/authentication/jett-auth) middleware on protected routes
- **DePIN earning** — staked JTX earns OPTX rewards (see [DePIN](/docs/infrastructure/depin))
- **Governance** — SPACE COWBOY tier unlocks governance voting
