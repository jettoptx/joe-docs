JettChat doesn't require a Solana wallet. If you'd rather pay USD, two payment rails get you the same access as the [staking tiers](/docs/token/tiers).

## Base entry — $8.88

Either rail clears the **base entry** gate (equivalent to holding 1 JTX):

- **Stripe** — *Pay $8.88 — no wallet needed*. Standard card checkout from the JETT Hub login surface.
- **[Tempo CLI](https://github.com/jettoptx/tempo)** — *Pay with Tempo CLI · $8.88*. Terminal-based payment from the same surface.

Both are 30-day basic gates by default.

## Subscription tiers — monthly billing

The three named tiers below are priced to match the JTX stake amounts at the canonical exchange rate. Pay USD/month and get the same access as the equivalent stake.

| Tier | Subscription | JTX-stake equivalent |
|------|--------------|----------------------|
| **MOJO** | $8.88 / mo | 12 JTX for 1 year |
| **DOJO** | $28.88 / mo | 444 JTX for 2 years |
| **SPACE COWBOY** | $88.88 / mo | 1,111 JTX for lifetime |

See [Tiers](/docs/token/tiers) for the full feature breakdown of each.

## Choosing stake vs. subscribe

- **Stake JTX** — best for users who plan to use JettChat sustained. JTX is also a network asset — staked JTX earns OPTX, governance power, and future airdrop eligibility.
- **Subscribe USD** — best for users who want JettChat without a Solana wallet, or who want shorter commitment than the staking durations.

The middleware does not care which path you took — gate decisions are made on access tier, not the source of access.

## Cancellation and downgrades

Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time; access continues through the end of the billing period. Stake locks are time-bounded by the staking page's published durations (1 year / 2 years / lifetime); they auto-release at expiry.
