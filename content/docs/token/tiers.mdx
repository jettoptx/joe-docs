## Base entry to JettChat

Pick **any one**:

- Hold **1 JTX** in your connected Solana wallet (cheapest sustained path)
- Pay **$8.88** via Stripe (no wallet needed)
- Pay **$8.88** via [Tempo CLI](https://github.com/jettoptx/tempo)

Base entry unlocks JettChat in either [xChat Native](/docs/jettchat/xchat-native) or [Phantom Mode](/docs/jettchat/phantom-mode).

## Subscription tiers

Each tier is accessible by **either** staking JTX or paying USD per month.

| Tier | JTX stake | Stake duration | Subscription | Earns | Headline unlocks |
|------|-----------|----------------|--------------|-------|-------------------|
| **MOJO** *(Starter)* | 12 JTX | 1 Year | $8.88 / mo | 12 OPTX/mo | Jett Native keyboard, Jett Augment customizations, Hub gaze keyboard + emoji packs, unlimited DOJO training, beta app access |
| **DOJO** *(Popular)* | 444 JTX | 2 Years | $28.88 / mo | 444 OPTX/mo @ 2× fiat | Custom emoji creation, Augment Net Graph with AR navigation, priority [AARON Router](https://github.com/jettoptx/joe-aaron-router) access, DePIN node eligibility |
| **SPACE COWBOY** *(Legendary)* | 1,111 JTX | Lifetime | $88.88 / mo | Unlimited OPTX mints | Full Jett Augment suite, complete AGT tensor pipeline, founder-tier AARON access, governance voting, future airdrop eligibility |

## Code-level enforcement

JettChat's middleware enforces these tiers via four code-level gate checks:

| Code tier | Threshold | Maps to |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| `basic` | ≥ 1 JTX held | Base entry |
| `mojo` | ≥ 12 JTX held | MOJO tier |
| `dojo` | ≥ 444 JTX held | DOJO tier |
| `spaceCowboy` | ≥ 1,111 JTX held | SPACE COWBOY tier |

The same balance is checked via Helius RPC on protected routes. See [`jettoptx/joe-jettchat-app/app/api/jtx-check`](https://github.com/jettoptx/joe-jettchat-app) for the implementation.
