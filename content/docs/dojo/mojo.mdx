**MOJO (Mobile Jett Optics)** is where users connect with their wallets, verify their identity through mobile biometrics, and use **Jett Cursor & Widgets** on their phone. It unlocks access to the full suite of DOJO augments and JOE orchestration tools.

## Pricing

Each tier is accessible by **either** staking JTX or paying USD/mo. The canonical numbers mirror [astroknots.space/stake](https://www.astroknots.space/stake) — see [Token / Tiers](/docs/token/tiers) for the full table.

| Tier | JTX stake | Duration | Subscription | Access |
|------|-----------|----------|--------------|--------|
| **MOJO** (Starter) | 12 JTX | 1 Year | $8.88 / mo | Full augment suite, JOE orchestration, HEDGEHOG gateway |
| **DOJO** (Popular) | 444 JTX | 2 Years | $28.88 / mo | Training ground, custom emoji creation, Augment Net Graph with AR navigation, priority AARON access, DePIN node eligibility |
| **SPACE COWBOY** (Legendary) | 1,111 JTX | Lifetime | $88.88 / mo | Everything + unlimited OPTX mints, full AGT tensor pipeline, founder-tier AARON access, governance voting, future airdrop eligibility |

## What MOJO Unlocks

- Full access to the **DOJO** training ground and all augments
- **AstroJOE** task orchestration and swarm decomposition
- **HEDGEHOG Gateway** AI routing (Grok + Claude)
- **Matrix relay** for agent-to-agent communication
- Priority support and early access to new augments
