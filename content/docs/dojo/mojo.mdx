**MOJO** is the operator subscription tier for OPTX. It unlocks access to the full suite of DOJO augments, AstroJOE orchestration tools, and the Cockpit dashboard.

## Pricing

| Tier | Price | Access |
|------|-------|--------|
| **DOJO** | $28.88 / 6 months | Training ground, knowledge map, basic augments |
| **MOJO** | $8.88 / month | Full augment suite, Cockpit, AstroJOE orchestration |
| **UNLIMITED** | $88.88 / month | Everything + priority validator node access + custom skills |

## What MOJO Unlocks

- Full access to the **DOJO** training ground and all augments
- **AstroJOE** task orchestration and swarm decomposition
- **OPTX Cockpit** live operator dashboard
- **HEDGEHOG Gateway** AI routing (Grok + Claude)
- **Matrix relay** for agent-to-agent communication
- Priority support and early access to new augments
